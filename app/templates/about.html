About The App:

TopCop was developed and implemeneted by Adam Jenkins. Data was obtained from the City of Boston's Crime Incident Report for the years of 2012-2014. Geolocation data was used based purely on the latitude and longitude given by the reporting incident officer and not with the X,Y coordinates. Additionaly use of Google's geolocation API was used to identify the latitude/longitude of the given address. Model predictions were made using Gaussian process regression using Scikit-Learn's module. This work was in association with the Insight Data Science program.
